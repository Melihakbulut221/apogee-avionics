Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

The things teams ask us before starting. Don’t see yours? Just ask.

Do I own the design files?

Yes. You receive the full source design package — schematics, PCB layout source, Gerbers, drill files, BOM, and assembly drawings. No vendor lock-in, ever.

What is your typical lead time?

You get a fixed-scope quote within one business day. Prototype boards typically take 2–6 weeks depending on complexity, layer count, and fabrication queue.

Will you sign an NDA?

Yes. We routinely work under NDA and treat your mission details and IP as strictly confidential. Send yours, or use ours.

Which tools and deliverables do you use?

We design in industry-standard EDA tools and deliver a complete, fab-ready package: schematics, layout source, Gerbers, drill files, BOM, pick-and-place files, and a functional test report.

How does payment work?

Projects are quoted and invoiced in USD with milestone-based payments — typically a deposit to begin and the balance on delivery. No hidden hourly billing.

Can you support export-controlled or ITAR programs?

We focus on commercial, educational, and research hardware. If your program involves export-controlled or ITAR-regulated items, tell us up front so we can scope it correctly or point you to the right partner.

Can you work with my existing team or contract manufacturer?

Absolutely. We hand off clean, documented design files and can coordinate directly with your CM, or manage fabrication and assembly for you — your choice.
